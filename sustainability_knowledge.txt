Water Conservation:
- Repair leaking taps immediately.
- Install sensor-based taps.
- Monitor daily water usage.

Energy Conservation:
- Use LED lighting.
- Turn off unused equipment.
- Install motion sensors.

Waste Management:
- Segregate biodegradable and non-biodegradable waste.
- Encourage recycling.
- Reduce single-use plastics.

Sustainable Campus Practices:
- Conduct environmental awareness programs.
- Promote paperless communication.
- Encourage public transportation.
